# Anchor Text SEO Optimization Guide

This document provides guidelines for creating SEO-optimized anchor text for Paramount Property Restoration website.

## ‚úÖ BEST PRACTICES

### 1. **Use Descriptive, Keyword-Rich Anchor Text**
**Bad:** Click Here | Read More | Learn More | View All
**Good:** Kitchen Remodeling Florida | Water Damage Restoration Services | Free Mold Inspection

### 2. **Include Location Keywords**
**Examples:**
- "Water Damage Restoration Orlando"
- "Kitchen Remodeling Services in Tampa"
- "Emergency Mold Remediation Florida"

### 3. **Be Specific About the Service**
**Bad:** Our Services | What We Do
**Good:** 24/7 Emergency Water Extraction | Professional Mold Removal & Remediation

### 4. **Natural Language that Makes Sense**
- Should read naturally in context
- Should describe what user will find
- Should include action or benefit when appropriate

## ‚ö†Ô∏è THINGS TO AVOID

1. **Over-optimization** - Don't stuff every link with exact match keywords
2. **Repetitive anchor text** - Vary the wording while keeping keywords
3. **Generic phrases** - Avoid "Click Here", "Read More", "Learn More"
4. **Vague descriptions** - Be specific about what the link leads to
5. **Too long anchors** - Keep it under 10 words when possible
