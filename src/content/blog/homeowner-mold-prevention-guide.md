## Why Florida Homes Are At Risk

Florida's warm, humid climate creates ideal conditions for mold growth. Understanding how to control moisture - the most critical factor - is your best defense against mold problems. This is why [water damage restoration](/restoration/water-damage) services are critical within the first 24-48 hours of exposure.

**Key Statistics:**
- Mold can grow within 24-48 hours of water exposure (immediate [emergency water removal](/restoration/water-damage/emergency-water-removal) is crucial)
- Indoor humidity above 55% accelerates mold growth
- The average Florida home has multiple mold hotspots requiring regular [mold inspections](/restoration/mold-remediation/mold-inspection)
